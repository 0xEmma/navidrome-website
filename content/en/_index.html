Navidrome is open source software distributed **free of charge** under the terms of the [GNU GPL v3](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0) license. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="light" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-bolt" title="Blazing fast!" url="/about" %}} Lightweight, fast and self-contained. Runs well even on resource-limited platforms (ex: Raspberry Pi) {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-database" title="Handles large libraries!" url="/about" %}} Plays well with gigantic music collections (tested with +152K songs!) {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-compress-arrows-alt" title="Transcoding on the fly!" url="/about" %}} Converts/reduces your music files as you play them, so you don't nuke your data plan {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-mobile-alt" title="Access your music from anywhere!" url="/about" %}} Choose from a large selection of mobile clients or use the integrated web player {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-certificate" title="Modern and up-to-date" url="/about" %}} Built with new technologies (GoLang, ReactJS), to ensure modern features and compatiblility {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-cogs" title="Subsonic API" url="/about" %}} Compatible with the de facto standard Music API, supporting all its clients and ecosystem {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-chart-line" title="Active project!" url="/about" %}} New releases adding features and bug fixes on a regular basis. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Contributions welcome!" url="https://github.com/deluan/navidrome" %}} We do a [Pull Request](https://github.com/deluan/navidrome/pulls) contributions workflow on **GitHub**.
