Translations are currently managed in [POEditor](https://poeditor.com/join/project/lkqJZfmKXe). 
If you want to contribute a new translations or help reviewing/proofreading any of the existing 
ones, please join the [translation project](https://poeditor.com/join/project/lkqJZfmKXe).
Also consider joining our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/xh7j7yF) for coordinating the 
translation efforts
